Target Platform: iOS

Brief Description: Official iOS application for HackIllinois 2017. HackIllnois is the annual Hackathon hosted by the University of Illinois. The app allows students from across the nation to login and have access to the schedule, locations of each events, annoucements, and a countdown timer for when the hackathon ends.
